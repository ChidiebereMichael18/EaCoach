About EA Coach

EA Coach helps you compare, book, and manage bus trips across Uganda and East Africa. We aggregate trusted bus operators so you can easily find schedules, prices, and pick your preferred seats — all in one place.

  • Coverage across all Ugandan districts and East African countries
  • Transparent pricing and real-time seat selection
  • Secure booking flow with multiple payment options

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my booking?

Changes depend on the bus operator policy. Check your booking in the dashboard and contact support if needed.

Which payment methods are supported?

You can pay using Debit/Credit Card, MTN MoMo, Airtel Mobile Money, and other local options.

Do I need to print my ticket?

A digital confirmation is usually enough. Some operators may require a reference code at boarding.

Need help with a booking?

Our team is here to assist you with routes, payments, and more.
